STEP 3. ACCEPTANCE OF ABSTRACT FOR THE ECS3

STEP 4. PAYMENT OF REGISTRATION FOR THE ECS3
Please pay registration fee. If your payment has not been received unit May 15 you will additionally be charged for late registration fee.
STEP 5. CONFORMATION OF ATTENDANCE TO THE ECS3
Your attendance to ECS3 will be finally confirmed in a e-mail message after your registration fee has been received. In order to speed up the procedure you may send a copy of the bank transfer by e-mail to jpopovic@irb.hr
STEP 6. PAYMENT OF THE ACCOMMODATION FOR THE ECS3
Soon after the conformation of attendance you will receive additional e-mail with the invoice for payment of the accommodation option you have chosen in Registration form. Payment information will be included. Please pay accommodation accordingly.
STEP 7. SEE YOU IN BOL FOR ECS3
Before processing your registration you are kindly invited to read the information about registration fees. Bursaries provided by IUCr will be available to cover partially or totally the travel and subsistence expenses. For more information visit bursary paragraph.
